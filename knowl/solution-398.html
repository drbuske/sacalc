Solution 6.2.7.12.1.

Place the cone so that the vertex of the cone is at the origin and the axis of the cone lies on the \(y\)-axis of the coordinate system. Then, the function defining the side of the cone is given by \(f(x) = \frac{h}{r}x\text{.}\) Using shells, the volume of the solid formed by rotating \(f(x)\) about the \(y\)-axis is given by
\begin{equation*} V = 2\pi \int_0^r x \left( h-\frac{h}{r}x \right) \ dx = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h\text{.} \end{equation*}
