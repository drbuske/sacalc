Exercise 14.

Suppose \(a > 0\) and that \(f(x) > 0\) for \(0 \leq x \leq a\text{.}\) Consider the region enclosed by the graphs of \(y=f(x)\text{,}\) \(y=1+f(x)\text{,}\) \(x=0\text{,}\) and \(x=a\text{.}\)

  1. What is the volume of the solid generated when this region is rotated around the \(y\)-axis?

  2. Suppose now that \(b < 0\text{.}\) What is the volume of the solid generated when this region is rotated around the axis \(x=b\text{?}\)
