Yes, it is true. Since \(a_2 \leq r \cdot a_1\text{,}\) we also have \(a_3 \leq r \cdot a_2 \leq r^2 \cdot a_1\) and so on until \(a_{k+1} \leq r^k \cdot a_1\text{.}\) Since \(\sum r^k \cdot a_1\) is a convergent geometric series, by the Direct Comparison Test, \(\sum a_k\) is also convergent.
