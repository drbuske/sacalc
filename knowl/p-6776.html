Consider the region \(S\) bounded by \(y=\sqrt{x}\text{,}\) the \(y\)-axis, and the line \(y=2\text{.}\) If we revolve \(S\) around the \(y\)-axis, which of the following integrals represents the application of the disk method? Which represents the application of the shell method? Draw diagrams to defend your responses. [Both integrals evaluate to \(\displaystyle \frac{32}{5}\pi\text{.}\)]

  • \(\displaystyle \displaystyle 2\pi \int_0^4 x(2-\sqrt{x}) \ dx\)

  • \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \pi \int_0^2 y^4 \ dy \)
