\(f'(5)\) using the average rate of change formula on a small interval around 5. Let's try the interval from \(x = 5\) to \(x = 5 + 0.0001\text{.}\) Then we have
\begin{equation*} f'(5) \approx {f(5+0.0001) - f(5)\over 0.0001} = {3^{5.0001} - 3^{5}\over 0.0001} \approx 267, \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} f'(5) \approx {f(5+0.00001) - f(5)\over 0.00001} = {3^{5.00001} - 3^{5}\over 0.00001} \approx 267. \end{equation*}
This agrees with our previous result to one decimal place, so \(f'(5)\approx 267\) is a good estimate.
