WolframAlpha gives \(\frac{2}{\cos\left( \frac{x}{2}\right)+1}+C\text{.}\) GeoGebra returns \(\frac{-2}{\tan \left(\frac{1}{2}x\right)+1}+C\text{.}\) These are not actually equal; however, they do differ by a constant. That is, \(\frac{2}{\cot\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)+1} - 2 = \frac{-2}{\tan\left( \frac{x}{2}\right) + 1}\text{.}\)
