Exercise 11.

Let \(\{A_n\}_{n=3}^{\infty}\) be a sequence whose terms represent the area of a regular polygon with \(n\) sides, with the distance from the centroid of the polygon to each corner equal to \(1\text{.}\)

  1. By dividing the polygon into \(n\) triangles, give a formula for \(A_n\text{.}\)

  2. What is \(\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} A_n\text{?}\)
