Item f.

We now so that the series \(\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{k-2}{k^4+1}\) is convergent by showing the sequence of partial sums \(\{S_n\}\) converge. Suppose that \(T = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} T_n\text{.}\) Then, since each term in the partial sum \(T_n\) is positive, we have that \(\{ T_n \}\) is an increasing sequence and \(T_n \leq T\) for all \(n\text{.}\) Show that the sequence \(\{S_n\}\) is monotone and bounded and hence also converges.
