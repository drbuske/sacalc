Solution 6.5.5.3.1.

\(w = 1 + e^{3 x}\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} \int_{-\infty}^{-2}\,{e^{3 x}\over 1 + e^{3 x}}\,dx = \lim\limits_{b\to 1^{+}} \int_b^{1 + e^{-6}} {1\over 3}{dw\over w} = \lim\limits_{b\to 1^{+}} {1\over 3}(\ln(1 + e^{-6}) - \ln(b)) = {1\over 3}\ln(1 + e^{-6}). \end{equation*}
