Let \(\{a_n\}\text{,}\) \(\{b_n\}\text{,}\) and \(\{c_n\}\) be sequences with \(\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} a_n = A\text{,}\) \(\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} b_n = B\text{,}\) and \(\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} c_n = C\text{.}\) Assume \(A, B\text{,}\) and \(C\) are nonzero real numbers. Evaluate the limits of the following sequences.

  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{a_n - b_n}{c_n}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{c_n}{n}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{a_{2n+5}}{b_n}\)
