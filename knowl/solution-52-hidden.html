(a) The statement \(f(150) = 375\) means that it costs 375 dollars to produce 150 gallons of ice cream. (b) The statement \(f'(150) = 1.6\) means that when the number of gallons produced is 150, costs are increasing by 1.6 dollars/gallon In other words, it costs about 1.6 dollars to produce the 151st gallon of ice cream.
