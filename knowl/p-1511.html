In Figure 1.8.2, we see the graph of a function \(f\) and its tangent line at the point \((a,f(a))\text{.}\) Notice how when we zoom in we see the local linearity of \(f\) more clearly highlighted. The function and its tangent line are nearly indistinguishable up close. Local linearity can also be seen dynamically in the java applet at http://gvsu.edu/s/6J 25 .
