We first get a feel for what we expect the series to do. For very large \(k\text{,}\) \(\frac{3k+2}{4k^2-3}\) behaves like \(\frac{3k}{4k^2} = \frac{3}{4k} = \frac{3}{4} \cdot \frac{1}{k}\text{.}\) Since \(\sum \frac{1}{k}\) diverges, then \(\sum \frac{3}{4k}\) also diverges, so we expect \(\sum \frac{3k+2}{4k^2 -3}\) to diverge as well.
