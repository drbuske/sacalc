Exercise 10. A Teardrop.

The "teardrop" is a curve parameterized by

\begin{equation*} x=2a\cos t - a\sin (2t), \ y=b\sin t, \ 0 \leq t < 2\pi \end{equation*}

where \(a\) and \(b\) are constants defining the shape.

  1. For your choice of positive constants \(a\) and \(b\text{,}\) use the applet https://www.geogebra.org/m/yJNhQMQa 29  to sketch a teardrop curve.

  2. Determine the area enclosed by the general teardrop curve.
