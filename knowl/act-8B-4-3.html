Activity 8.4.4.

  1. Using a known Taylor series, find the first four nonzero terms of the Maclaurin series for \(\arcsin (x)\text{.}\)

  2. Find the sum of the series \(\sum_{p=1}^{\infty} px^{p-1}\) for \(|x| < 1\text{.}\)

  3. Find the Maclaurin series for \(f(x) = \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{2}\text{.}\) From the series, can you determine if the function \(f(x)\) is even or odd? Based on its Taylor series, is \(f'(x)\) even or odd? How about \(f''(x)\text{?}\)
