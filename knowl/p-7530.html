Assuming all the limits on the right-hand side exist:

  • If \(b\) is a constant, then \(\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} (bs_n) = b \left( \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n \right)\)

  • \(\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} (s_n + t_n) = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n + \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} t_n\)

  • \(\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} (s_n \cdot t_n) = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n \cdot \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} t_n\)

  • \(\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \left( \frac{s_n}{t_n} \right) = \frac{\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n}{ \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} t_n}\text{,}\) provided \(\displaystyle \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} t_n \neq 0\)

  • For any constant \(a\text{,}\) \(\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} a = a\)
