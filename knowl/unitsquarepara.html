Exercise 10. Parameterizing a Square.

Imagine a particle that starts at time \(t=0\text{,}\) ends at time \(t=10\text{,}\) and moves counterclockwise around the unit square parameterized by
\begin{equation*} x=f(t), y=g(t) \end{equation*}
as shown in Figure 9.1.18. Sketch possible graphs of \(f\) and \(g\text{.}\) For simplicity, assume the motion is of a constant speed on each edge of the square.
