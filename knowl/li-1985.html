Item a.

Consider the curve \(r=2+2\cos\theta\text{.}\)

  1. If \((r,\theta)\) is on the graph, will \((r,-\theta)\) also be on the graph? Is the graph symmetric with respect to the \(x\)-axis?

  2. If \((r,\theta)\) is on the graph, will \((-r,-\theta)\) also be on the graph? Is the graph symmetric with respect to the \(y\)-axis?

  3. If \((r,\theta)\) is on the graph, will \((-r,\theta)\) also be on the graph? Is the graph symmetric with respect to the origin?

  4. Sketch the graph of \(r=2+2\cos\theta\text{.}\)
