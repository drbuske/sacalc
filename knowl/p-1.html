I am especially thankful for the text Active Calculus. This text is heavily based upon Active Calculus 4  and I have borrowed liberally from the text under the CC BY-SA 4.0 License. For a fair portion of the last chapters, much material has also been adapted liberally from Coordinated Calculus 5 , CLP-2 Integral Calculus 6  and OpenStax Calculus 7 . This text is largely a compilation of materials from these sources (with a few of my own added as well). Many thanks are thus in order for all the contributing authors for these projects, but especially to Matt Boelkins, Nate Wakefield, Joel Feldman, Gilbert Strang, and Edwin "Jed" Herman. None of this work would be possible without standing on the shoulders of so many others.
