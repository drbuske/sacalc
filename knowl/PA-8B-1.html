Preview Activity 8.1.1.

Consider the power series

\begin{equation*} f(x)=\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^k}{2^k}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  1. Determine the values of \(f(1)\) and \(f \left( \frac{3}{2} \right)\text{.}\)

  2. For some values of \(x\text{,}\) this is a geometric series. For these values, find a general formula for the sum and hence \(f(x)\text{.}\)

  3. Determine the values for which this power series converges.
