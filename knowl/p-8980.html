Since the power series is centered at \(x=-2\text{,}\) the fact that the series converges at \(x=2\) tells us that the radius of convergence is greater than or equal to \(4\text{,}\) since \(4\) is the distance between \(2\) and \(-2\text{.}\) Since \(-5\) is \(3\) away from \(-2\) the series will converge at \(x=-5\text{.}\)
