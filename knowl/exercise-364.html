Exercise 7.

Consider the series

\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{k}{3^k}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  1. Use the ratio test to show that the series converges.

  2. Use summation by parts,

    \begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^n a_k (b_{k+1}-b_k) = \left[ a_{n+1}b_{n+1} - a_1b_1 \right] - \sum_{k=1}^n b_{k+1}(a_{k+1}-a_k) \end{equation*}

    to find the sum of the series. Hint: Take \(a_k = k\) and \(b_k = 3^{1-k}/2\text{.}\)
