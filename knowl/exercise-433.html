Exercise 8. Examining Path and Speed of a Particle.

A particle moves in the \(xy\)-plane so that its position at time \(t\) is given by \(x=\sin (2t)\text{,}\) \(y=-\cos (2t)\) for \(0 \leq t < 2\pi\text{.}\)

  1. At what time does the particle first touch the \(x\)-axis? What is the speed of the particle at that time?

  2. Is the particle ever at rest?
