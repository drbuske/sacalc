  1. The integral \(2\pi\int_0^4 x(2-\sqrt{x}) \ dx\) represents the application of the shell method. The integral \(\pi \int_0^2 y^4 \ dy\) represents the application of the disk method.

    1. \(\displaystyle V=2\pi\int_0^4 y(2-\sqrt{y}) \ dy = \frac{32\pi}{5}\)

    2. \(\displaystyle V = \pi\int_1^3 (x-1)^4 \ dx = \frac{32\pi}{5}\)

    1. \(\displaystyle V=\pi \int_0^1 (2-y^2)^2 - (2-\sqrt{y})^2 \ dy = \frac{31\pi}{30}\)

    2. \(\displaystyle V=2\pi \int_0^1 (2-x)(\sqrt{x}-x^2) \ dx = \frac{31\pi}{30}\)
