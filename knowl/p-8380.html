Even when possible, applying the integral test can be difficult due to integration involved. We can in many cases instead use the direct comparison test to compare the convergence of a challenging series with a much simpler \(p\)-series. In particular, if \(0 \leq a_k \leq b_k\) for all \(k\text{,}\) then

  1. If \(\sum b_k\) converges, then \(\sum a_k\) also converges.

  2. If \(\sum a_k\) diverges, then \(\sum b_k\) also diverges.
