For example, the series

\begin{equation*} 1+(x-2)+(x-2)^2 + (x-2)^3 + \cdots \end{equation*}

is a power series centered at \(x=2\text{,}\) with \(c_k=1\) for \(k \geq 0\text{,}\) and a geometric series

\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} bx^k = b + bx + bx^2 + bx^3 + \cdots = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} b(x-0)^k \end{equation*}

is a power series centered at \(x=0\) with \(c_k = b\) for \(k \geq 0\text{.}\)
