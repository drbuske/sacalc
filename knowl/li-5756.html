Take the tangent of both sides. This gives \(\tan\theta = \tan(\pi/3) = \sqrt{3}\text{.}\) Since \(\tan\theta = \frac{y}{x}\) we can replace the left-hand side of this equation by \(\frac{y}{x}\text{.}\) This gives \(\frac{y}{x}=\sqrt{3}\text{,}\) which can be rewritten as \(y=\sqrt{3}x\text{.}\)
